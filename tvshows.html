How to Get Away With Murder

Release Date: September 25, 2014

Summary of the first season:

  • At Middleton University in Philadelphia, Annalise Keating is a prominent criminal defense attorney and law professor. Five of her first year students are chosen for internships in her company: Wes Gibbins, Connor Walsh, Michaela Pratt, Asher Millstone, and Laurel Castillo (The Keating 5). They work with staff of Annalise, an assistant counsel, Frank Delfino and Bonnie Winterbottom. When the first season reveals Keating's sporadic clients, in both flashback and flashforward scenes, it explores two similar murders: Lila Stangard, Annalise's husband's mistress and a Middleton pupil, and then Sam Keating, Annalise's husband, who was murdered by Annalise's interns.
